What We Do

We provide Non-Domestic Commercial EPC's for most properties including but not limited to restaurants, pubs, shops, offices, factories, industrial units and medial centres. We can normally attend the next day and aim to have the certificate ready for lodgement 48 hours after the survey has been completed. It may be possible to lodge the EPC the next day if required urgently. Unlike some of the panel EPC companies that just act as a booking service, if your property does not meet the minimum energy efficiency standard, we will not automatically lodge the EPC, but first advise on how to improve the rating so any improvements can be made before the EPC is lodged. This saves you the cost of commissioning a second EPC if the first has failed. We are accredited with Elmhurst Energy and hold public and professional liability insurance as well as being DBS checked. We cover London, Hertfordshire, Bedfordshire, Oxfordshire, Essex and Buckinghamshire for all your non-domestic EPC requirements.

Property Survey

Our comprehensive property surveys provide a detailed analysis of the energy efficiency of your commercial building. We evaluate energy consumption and identify areas for improvement, ensuring that your property meets regulatory standards. With our expert recommendations, you can enhance your building's performance while reducing energy costs and environmental impact.

Gas Certificate

Our certified gas engineers conduct thorough inspections of your property’s gas systems to ensure safety and compliance with UK regulations. We issue Gas Safety Certificates (CP12) for commercial properties, confirming that all gas installations are safe, properly maintained, and meet industry standards. This service ensures the safety of occupants and helps you avoid legal complications.

Electric Certificate

We provide professional electrical inspections and certification services to guarantee the safety and compliance of your property's electrical systems. Our electricians carry out detailed assessments and issue Electric Certificates to confirm that your installations are in line with current safety regulations. Regular inspections help prevent potential hazards and ensure your property’s electrical integrity.

Floor Plan

Our accurate and detailed floor plans offer a comprehensive visual representation of your commercial property’s layout. These plans are essential for space planning, building assessments, and compliance with legal and safety standards. Whether you're renovating or leasing your property, our floor plans provide clear and precise documentation to meet your needs.

Quote List

We offer transparent and itemized quote lists for all our services, giving you a clear breakdown of costs upfront. This ensures that you can plan and budget effectively, with no hidden fees or surprises. Our quote list helps you make informed decisions by outlining the specific costs associated with surveys, certifications, and retrofits.

Commercial Retrofit

Our commercial retrofit services focus on modernizing your building's energy systems to improve efficiency and reduce operational costs. We assess your current systems and recommend energy-efficient upgrades, such as enhanced insulation, modern heating systems, and optimized lighting. By retrofitting your property, you not only lower energy consumption but also increase the overall value and sustainability of your building.
